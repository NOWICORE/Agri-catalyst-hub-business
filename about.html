About Agri-Catalyst Hub

Leading Agri-Solutions Provider

Established on May 13, 2025, Agri-Catalyst Hub is a private consultancy firm registered in Malawi, dedicated to transforming agriculture through sustainable and scalable solutions.

Our vision is to empower agricultural producers and Agri-enterprises across Malawi with climate-smart technologies and agribusiness development, fostering resilience and inclusive rural growth.

Multidisciplinary team with expertise in agronomy and engineering.

Turnkey solutions from diagnostics to deployment.

Focus on low-cost, locally adaptable technologies.
